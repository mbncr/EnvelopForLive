Envelop for Live 10.0.0
Apr 18, 2018
----------------------------------------------------------------------------
The official first release of Envelop for Live 10! This new version of
Envelop for Live takes advantage of the multi-channel audio routing features
of Max for Live in version 10, enabling full Ambisonic spatial audio
processing and decoding entirely within Live 10.
